The Process

How Shajar Works

A transparent, three-step process to make sure every rupee reaches the right person.

1
📋

Students Apply

Deserving university students submit verified academic and financial details through our transparent platform.

2
🔍

We Verify

Our team carefully reviews each application ensuring complete transparency and fairness in the selection process.

3
🎓

Donors Fund Directly

Funds transfer directly to the institution's finance office — never to personal accounts — guaranteeing safe delivery.

What We Offer

Our Programs

Two focused initiatives to give students a real chance at a brighter future.

🎓

Scholarship Fund

Full and partial tuition scholarships for high-achieving students from low-income families at accredited Pakistani universities.

💼

Interest-Free Loans

Qarz-e-hasana style interest-free loans to help students cover semester fees, books, and essential living costs.

🌍

Global Donor Network

Connecting diaspora donors worldwide with verified students back home — simple, trusted, and impactful giving.

📈

Career Mentorship

Pairing funded students with industry mentors who guide them toward internships, placements, and professional growth.
