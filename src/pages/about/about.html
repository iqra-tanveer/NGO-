Who We Are

About Shajar

A modern non-profit initiative focused on empowering extraordinary individuals through education and financial support — helping people grow, just like a tree, by building stronger futures from the ground up.

Purpose

What Drives Us

🌱

Our Mission

To provide interest-free financial assistance and secure scholarships to deserving individuals so they can achieve stability, excellent education, and long-term professional success — with complete dignity.

🌍

Our Vision

A world where financial hardship never prevents talented and driven individuals from achieving their full potential and contributing positively to global society and their communities.

Transparency First

Our Financial Transparency Policy

All infrastructure, server, and administrative costs are privately funded by our founding members. We never deduct a percentage from general donations.
We never transfer money to students' personal accounts. All funds are wired directly to the educational institution's official finance office.
